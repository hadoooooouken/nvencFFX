nvencFFX 1.5.4

Video converter and trimming tool powered by NVIDIA NVENC hardware acceleration.

Features:
• Hardware-accelerated video encoding and decoding
• Support for H.264, HEVC (H.265), and AV1 codecs
• Visual trimming with start/end time controls
• Resolution scaling and frame rate adjustment
• Custom audio codec, bitrate, and channel settings
• Live FFmpeg command preview and size estimation
• Advanced encoder parameters and presets
• Real-time process output and encoding progress
• Save and load configuration profiles

System Requirements:
• NVIDIA GPU with NVENC support (GeForce 1000 series or newer)
• FFmpeg with NVENC support
• Windows 10/11
